code-block is an open source fastn code-block component. It's built using fastn.

code-block is part of fastn-community and built one contribution at a time by community members just like you. You can contribute for code-block in many forms beyond just writing code, from reporting an issue or suggesting a new feature to improving documentation or providing feedback on proposed changes.
